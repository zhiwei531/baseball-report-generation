# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Youth baseball athletes and parents use the player report to understand strengths, priorities, and practical drills. Coaches use the comparison and metric views to plan training. Researchers and engineers use the research view to audit Vicon, 2D-video, event-detection, and derived-metric evidence.

## Product Purpose

Generate reproducible batting and pitching reports from Vicon C3D and synchronized 2D video. A successful report preserves the approved final template, clearly separates player, coach, and researcher views, identifies the correct athlete throughout, and traces each conclusion back to a metric, event frame, or source artifact.

## Brand Personality

Clinical, clear, and trustworthy. The report should feel like a professional sports assessment, with plain-language guidance for families and sufficient methodological detail for researchers.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic marketing dashboards, decorative data visualization, cramped two-column research charts, unreadably small chart labels, athlete-name leakage from prior templates, and polished placeholders that appear to be measured results.

## Design Principles

- Preserve the approved final report structure and reading order across athletes.
- Make evidence legible before increasing information density.
- Keep batting and pitching generation reproducible from documented inputs and parameters.
- Separate measured values, automatic candidate events, and manually confirmed settings.
- Keep player-facing guidance simple while retaining traceability in researcher sections.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast for HTML output. Do not rely on color alone for status or comparison meaning. Keep chart labels readable at normal desktop zoom, preserve semantic headings and image alternative text, and ensure the report remains usable when responsive layouts collapse to one column.
